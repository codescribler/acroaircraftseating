How We Work

Three Steps to Seating That Transforms Your Airline

From your vision to the cabin, a partnership built on expertise and collaboration.

1

Share Your Vision

We start by listening. What does your brand need? What are your passengers experiencing? What operational constraints matter most? This isn't a brief — it's a conversation where we become experts in your airline, your routes, your goals. Then we translate that vision into a detailed engineering specification.

2

We Design & Engineer Together

Your cabin team, our design engineers, and our manufacturing experts collaborate iteratively. CAD renders, material samples, mock-ups, compliance reviews — we move at pace but never sacrifice quality. You have veto power at every stage. This is co-creation, not compromise.

3

We Deliver & Support for Life

Manufacturing begins. Your seats are built to specification at our UK HQ, tested rigorously, and delivered on schedule. Then AcroCare™ kicks in — your dedicated Key Account Manager, proactive spares forecasting, technical support, engineering updates, and a partnership that lasts for the entire life of the fleet.
